Introduction

This project consists of four major sections, which eventually leads to risk assessment of structures by investing the effects of cracks in exposed members.

Image Processing

Traits of visible cracks in members are detected by the use of image processing techniques. Length, position and width of cracks are considered in this process.

Frequency Estimation

The effects of detected cracks from the previous section are measured by the fundamental principles of structural dynamics and charectaristic equation.

Health Monitoring

Due to uncertainties in properties of cracks, a probabilistic health monitoring procedure is defined which results in the statistical distribution of the stiffness of members.

Risk Analysis

By the use of deteremined features of structural random variables, reliabilty methods can be utilized to calculate the probabilty of failure of structure. This can be used prior to taking impulsive actions.

Services

Services provided by this website consists of crack detection, structural properties measurement, and risk assessment of the system, which eases the process of decision making.
